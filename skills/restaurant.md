# Restaurant Skill

Use this skill when the image analysis indicates food, dining, cafes, restaurants, menus, or table settings.

## Matching Signals

- Food, drinks, plates, bowls, trays, cutlery, table settings
- Menus, receipts, ordering kiosks, restaurant signage
- Cafe, bakery, bar, dining room, counter, or kitchen context
- Keywords such as restaurant, cafe, meal, dessert, coffee, menu, dish, table

## Writing Rules

- Emphasize taste, texture, atmosphere, price/value, and visit context.
- Avoid unsupported claims about ingredients, health effects, or business details.
- Include practical visitor details when available.
- Mention uncertainty when flavor, price, location, or service details are inferred.

## Required Sections

- Title
- Short intro
- Food or menu observations
- Atmosphere and use case
- Visit tips when supported
- SEO tags

## Boundaries

- Do not invent restaurant names, exact prices, opening hours, or addresses.
- Do not claim health benefits unless provided by verified source data.
- Do not describe taste as fact unless the source includes tasting notes.
