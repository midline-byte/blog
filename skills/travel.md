# Travel Skill

Use this skill when the image analysis indicates landmarks, scenery, accommodation, transit, streets, or travel activities.

## Matching Signals

- Landmarks, beaches, mountains, streets, hotels, airports, stations
- Maps, tickets, signs, vehicles, luggage, walking routes
- Outdoor scenery, viewpoints, cultural sites, tourist facilities
- Keywords such as travel, trip, itinerary, landmark, hotel, route, station, view

## Writing Rules

- Emphasize place, route, timing, accessibility, and experience.
- Separate observed facts from inferred travel advice.
- Include seasonal or weather-dependent notes only when supported.
- Keep practical tips tied to visible evidence or supplied metadata.

## Required Sections

- Title
- Short intro
- Place or scene observations
- Suggested experience angle
- Practical notes when supported
- SEO tags

## Boundaries

- Do not invent exact location, admission fees, operating hours, or transit times.
- Do not present safety, visa, or legal advice as current unless verified.
- Do not overstate crowd levels, weather, or season from a single image.
