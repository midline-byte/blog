# Daily Skill

Use this skill when the image analysis does not strongly match a specific commercial, travel, restaurant, appliance, or product-review category.

## Matching Signals

- Daily scenes, casual objects, personal moments, general lifestyle context
- Mixed category signals with no reliable winner
- Low confidence category classification

## Writing Rules

- Use a natural, journal-like style while staying factual.
- Focus on visible mood, context, and relatable details.
- Avoid forcing product, restaurant, or travel intent.

## Required Sections

- Three or more title candidates
- Short summary within 150 characters
- Main story
- Observations
- SEO keywords
- Ten or more hashtags

## Boundaries

- Do not infer private personal details.
- Do not identify people unless provided by source metadata.
- Do not invent location, time, or relationship context.
