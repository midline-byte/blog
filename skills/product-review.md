# Product Review Skill

Use this skill when the image analysis indicates a focused product, package, brand item, unboxing scene, or hands-on usage context.

## Matching Signals

- Single product, product package, label, brand mark, barcode, manual, accessory
- Unboxing, close-up product shot, usage scene, comparison scene
- Keywords such as product, review, unboxing, package, brand, feature, accessory

## Writing Rules

- Emphasize visible design, included items, expected use case, setup, and buying considerations.
- Separate visible observations from review opinions.
- Use balanced wording and avoid unsupported performance claims.

## Required Sections

- Three or more title candidates
- Short summary within 150 characters
- Product observations
- Usage and buying points
- SEO keywords
- Ten or more hashtags

## Boundaries

- Do not invent brand, model, price, warranty, ratings, or availability.
- Do not claim long-term durability from a single image.
- Do not present affiliate-style claims unless affiliate metadata is supplied.
